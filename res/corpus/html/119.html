Picks


Usman v Covington
Usman, DEC
Usman dominated Woodley, RDA, and Maia. Covington barely won the RDA fight (a few analysts even had it for RDA); he got lit up by Maia in round 1 and bit of round 2. His Lawler win was a bit overblown imo. Lawler is done, no? He lost 50/45 against RDA as well.

Holloway v Volkanovski
Holloway, DEC
Holloway is just too ******' good. He won't get held down by Volkanovski. His boxing is superior. His chin is iron clad. And, his reach and height advantage puts it over the edge. Not picking a finish as both fighters have insane cardio, and that's the way Holloway usually ends his opponents.

Nunes v GDR
GDR, DEC
GDR will play it safe, keep it standing and it'll end up being a boring kickboxing match.


Aldo v Moraes
Aldo, TKO r2
They're the same fighter, except this is ALDO! If he can make weight (or if he weighs in over, and doesn't even try), I think he'll smash Moraes.

Yan v Faber
Yan, anyway he wants
DUh

Perry v Neal
Neal, DEC
Neal is a far more technical striker. THe only x factor will be whether Perry can somehow catch Neal with his power late in the fight. I don't think he'll do it.

UFC 245: Usman Vs Covington

Kamaru Usman made his UFC debut in 2015 and proved to the world just how good of a fighter he was when he defeated his first opponent Leon Edwards. A year later, he was scheduled to face Sérgio Moraes at UFC 198 but Usman was removed from the card due to undisclosed reasons. However, during the same year, he battled with Alexander Yakovlev and Warlley Alves, winning both fights. He then went on a nine-fight undefeated streak and became the UFC Welterweight Champion by defeating Tyron Woodley.

Colby Covington joined UFC in 2014 and showed everyone just how lethal he can get with his fists when he won his first fight against Anying Wang by TKO. He then went on to take on many top names of the sport inclduing Mike Pyle, Bryan Barberena, Dong Hyun Kim and Demian Maia and won against all of them. Covington then won the Interim UFC Welterweight Championship by defeating Rafael dos Anjos.

In which cities will the 2022 World Cup matches be held?

All 2022 World Cup matches will be held in stadiums in one of seven cities on Qatar’s eastern coast: Doha, Al Khor, Al Rayyan, Al Wakrah, Al Shamal, Umm Salal, and Lusail.

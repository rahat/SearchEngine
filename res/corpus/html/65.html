List of richest people in the world

This is a list of richest people in the world based on real-time assessment of wealth and assets by Forbes.[1] This list is limited to the top 20 individuals, with a total net worth exceeding $1.3 trillion U.S. dollars.The richest person in the world currently is Amazon founder and CEO, Jeff Bezos with a net worth of over $113 billion.[2] The richest woman in the world is L'Oréal heiress Françoise Bettencourt Meyers with a net worth of over $55.7 billion.[3]

UFC 245

UFC 245 live stream results and complete coverage for Kamaru Usman vs. Colby Covington-led pay-per-view (PPV) event on ESPN+ from inside T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada, airing Sat., Dec. 14, 2019. That's when "Nigerian Nightmare" and "Chaos" will attempt to end their contentious long-standing rivalry with the undisputed Welterweight title on the line. Elsewhere on the PPV card, Featherweight champion Max Holloway will battle Alexander Volkanovski, while women's Bantamweight queenpin, Amanda Nunes, tangles with Germaine de Randamie in a rematch six years in the making. That's right, UFC 245 is a championship tripleheader -- UFC is closing out 2019 with a bang! Get real-time UFC 245 updates, odds, start time, tickets, weigh ins, predictions, previews, video highlights, press conferences, winners, losers and much more right here before, during and well after the blockbuster show.

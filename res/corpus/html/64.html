      Who is the richest person in the world?

      In March 2019, Forbes released its annual billionaires list, which ranks the richest people on Earth by net worth. (There's a separate list just for women.)

      Here are the richest people on the planet for spring 2019.

      Credit: AP

    • 20. Ma Huateng (China)

      Chairman of Tencent Holdings

      Age: 47
      Net worth: $38.8 billion

      Credit: Lintao Zhang / Getty Images

    • 19. Steve Ballmer (U.S.)

      Former CEO of Microsoft; current owner of the Los Angeles Clippers

      Age: 62
      Net worth: $41.2 billion

      Credit: Stephen Dunn/Getty Images

    • 18. Rob Walton (U.S.)

      Chairman, Walmart

      Age: 74
      Net worth: $44.3 billion

      Credit: Sarah Bentham/AP

    • 17. Alice Walton (U.S.)

      Walmart heir; chair, Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art

      Age: 69
      Net worth: $44.4 billion

      Credit: Danny Johnston/AP

    • 16. Jim Walton (U.S.)

      Walmart heir; chairman and CEO, Arvest Bank Group, Inc.

      Age: 70
      Net worth: $44.6 billion

      Credit: Charles Sykes/AP

    • 15. Francoise Bettencourt Meyers & family (France)

      Chairwoman of family's L'Oreal holdings and granddaughter of L'Oreal's founder

      Age: 65
      Net worth: $49.3 billion

      Credit: Francois Guillot/AFP/Getty Images

    • 14. Sergey Brin (U.S.)

      President, Alphabet Inc.

      Age: 45
      Net worth: $49.8 billion

      Chairman, Reliance Industries

      Age: 61
      Net worth: $50 billion

      Age: 78

      Net worth: $50.5 billion

      CEO, Alphabet

      Age: 45
      Net worth: $50.8 billion

      Credit: Emmanuel Dunand/AFP/Getty Images

      Co-founder, Bloomberg

      Age: 77
      Net worth: $55.5 billion

UFC 245: Amanda Nunes-Holly Holm, Three Champs, Three Fights

The event will take place from Las Vegas and feature Kamaru Usman defending his title against Colby Covington and two more title fights.

Fightline
/
Published on Nov 28, 2019 at 8:18 AM
by Dana Becker
  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Pin It
  • Share

  • Print

The UFC will close out 2019 with a bang, presenting three championship matches at UFC 245.

The event takes place December 14 from the T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas.

In the main event, Kamaru Usman meets former interim champion Colby Covington for the UFC welterweight title. Also, Max Holloway puts his featherweight title on the line vs. top contender Alexander Volkanovski.

Completing the series of title fights will be double-champion Amanda Nunes defending her bantamweight belt against Germaine de Randamie, who was the inaugural featherweight titleholder. Nunes, who knocked out de Randamie in 2013, is also the featherweight champion.

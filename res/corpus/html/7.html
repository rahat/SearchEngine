The 2020 NBA All-Star Game will be an exhibition game played on February 16, 2020. NBA All Star Weekend that features the Three-Point Contest, Slam Dunk Contest, and Skills Challenge will be on February 15, 2020. It will be the 69th edition of the event. The game will be held at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois, home of the Chicago Bulls. This will be the third time that Chicago will host the All-Star Game. The last time the game was played there was in 1973 and 1988, at the Bulls' previous home arena Chicago Stadium. The game will be televised nationally by TNT for the 18th consecutive year.[1]

All-Star game[edit]

Coaches[edit]

The two teams will be coaching from their respective conferences for the Eastern Conference and the Western Conference. Coaches will be announced in late January.

Rosters[edit]

The entire roster will be released in mid January in a NBA All Star Announcement Special on TNT.

Draft[edit]

The draft will take place on February 6, 2020 on TNT.

Lineups[edit]

The lineups will be announced at a later date.

Game[edit]

The game will be played at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois on Sunday February 16, 2020 on TNT.

All-Star weekend[edit]

NBA On TNT American Express Road Show[edit]

The 2020 NBA All Star Weekend will begin on Thursday February 13, 2020 with the annual NBA on TNT American Express Road Show, along with a live broadcast of Inside the NBA will take place in Chicago, Illinois and will be hosted by Ernie Johnson Jr. Charles Barkley Kenny Smith and Shaquille O'Neal with live special performances to be announced at a later date.

Celebrity Game[edit]

Further information: NBA All-Star Weekend Celebrity Game

The 2020 NBA All-Star Celebrity Game which is expceted to be presented by Ruffles will be played on Friday February 14, 2020 at Wintrust Arena.

Rising Stars Challenge[edit]

Further information: Rising Stars Challenge

The 2020 NBA Rising Stars Challenge will be played on Friday February 14, 2020 at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois.

Skills Challenge[edit]

Further information: Skills Challenge

The 2020 Skills Challenge will be held on Saturday February 15, 2020 at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois.

Three Point Contest[edit]

Further information: Three Point Contest

The 2020 Three Point Contest will be held on Saturday February 15, 2020 at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois.

Slam Dunk Contest[edit]

Further information: Slam Dunk Contest

The 2020 Slam Dunk Contest will be held on Saturday February 15, 2020 at the United Center in Chicago, Illinois.

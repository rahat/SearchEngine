2020 NBA Title Odds Tracker: Los Angeles Leads The Way With Clippers, Lakers

NBA
Credit:

Credit: Kirby Lee-USA TODAY Sports

Evan Abrams
Nov 25, 2019, 10:05 AM EST
  • The Los Angeles Clippers are the favorites to win the 2020 title at PointsBet and DraftKings, followed by the Los Angeles Lakers.

The Clippers won the offseason and followed it up by entering the regular season as favorites to win their first NBA championship in franchise history.

With Los Angeles entering the year as the favorites, it broke the Warriors streak of three consecutive seasons entering the regular season as title favorites.

Right behind the Clippers is their close friend, the Los Angeles Lakers. The two teams from L.A. enter the regular season at the top of the odds board.

For the Clippers, it is the just the second time in the last 35 years they open a regular season under 10-1, doing so back in 2014-15. The Lakers on the other hand have lived in the spotlight for decades.

The team from the Eastern Conference with the lowest odds is the Milwaukee Bucks. Coming off their first 60-win season since 1980, the Bucks have serious expectations in 2020.
